## Overview

Action lists are essential for overlay components, being used by menus, selection panels, and other overlays. They can also be used directly in page sidebars for showing individual actions, for handling local navigation, and for displaying metadata.

Action lists can group items into sections, and support dividers and headers for establishing rhythm and hierarchy.

Action lists use a mobile-friendly inset style. Its items adapt to touch-based devices and its single-column format should work consistently in any screen size.
