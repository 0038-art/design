## Overview

Action lists can have many applications. They’re the foundation of menus, selection panels, and other overlay components; they can be applied to page sidebars for showing individual actions, for handling local navigation, and for displaying metadata.

Action lists support dividers and headers for grouping items into sections. They also support inset dividers between individual items for clarity.

Action lists use a mobile-friendly inset style. Their items adapt to touch-based devices, and their single-column format should render consistently in any screen size.
